# Warm
a minimalistic, futuristic and clean homepod that tells users the time, temperature, date and to-do tasks. Designed for productivity and organisation. 

**What is warm?**

Warm is a homepod that sits in your living room, kitchen, bedroom or wherever you live - and gives you important updates so you can stay up to date. Theese features include:
 - Tasks
 - Time
 - Date
 - Stopwatch
 - Timers
 - More Coming Soon
